About Us

Hola Amigos! We are a family owned authentic mexican restaurant called Vive Asada. We only use the freshest ingredients which come straight from our garden for our amazing customers. Grab your sombraros and finest bolo ties and come on down! Viva La Mexico!!

Spectacular Specials

Our famous birria tacos slow cooked to perfection will make you feel like you can run with the bulls. We only use the finest meat to create our popular dish. We raise our cattle with the utmost respect throughout their entire lives. Our cattle go for a morning walk, followed by a their afternoon massage, and finally they graze on the greenest of grass.
